Empowering the Albanian Diaspora Worldwide

Our goal is to create a global, user-driven platform that maps Albanian organizations by country, city, and activity, empowering communities to connect, share resources, and showcase their impact.

A New Digital Hub for a Global Community

Alrise is an initiative created to empower the Albanian diaspora by offering innovative, accessible and collaborative digital tools. Our mission is to promote visibility, foster collaboration and support the long-term growth of Albanian-led organizations worldwide.

How It Works

How the Global Albanian organization Map Works

Search & Explore

Use filters by country, city, or domain (culture, youth, sports, business, etc.) to discover active Albanian organizations worldwide.

View Organizations Profiles

Each profile includes a description, contact info, social media links, and areas of activity — all managed directly by the organizations themselves.

Add Your Organization

If your organization is not listed, simply fill out a quick registration form and join the global map. Approval is fast and easy.

Stay connected

Use the platform to follow updates, events, and new initiatives from the Albanian diaspora network.
